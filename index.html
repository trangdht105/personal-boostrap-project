Know where your child's milk comes from

High quality milk sourced from our own farms in the Netherlands. 150 years of our dairy expertise is used to create the ideal nourishing environment for producing high quality milk.

Grow together to nurture children and mums, naturally

Our farmers specially select cows based on their health, milk quality and milk composition. Using neck sensors, our farmers monitor each cow’s health through their daily activity, such as how many steps they take, how long they lie down and how much they eat.
Through this, our farmers are able to monitor the quality of milk to get high quality NOVAS Signature Milk.

The characteristics of NOVAS Signature Milk

The result of this craftsmanship is nutritious, high quality milk which contains small molecules with soft structure#
With its distinct characteristic of small molecules with soft structure*, NOVAS Signature Milk has low casein mineralisation. Milk with low casein mineralisation is known to form soft curd particles that are easily digested in the stomach^.
